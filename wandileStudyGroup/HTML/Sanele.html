Background and Biography

Sanele Khanyile was born and raised in Pietermaritzburg, KwaZulu-Natal. He moved to Pretoria to study BIT in Information Systems at the University of Pretoria, where he developed a strong interest in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. He sees technology as a way to solve complex global problems, and he hopes to one day work in ethical hacking and data protection.

Hobbies

Sanele is an avid gamer, always looking for new challenges in competitive and open-world games. When he’s not gaming, he enjoys coding, working on small passion projects that push his technical skills to new limits. Outside of technology, he loves hiking in nature reserves, enjoying the peacefulness of the outdoors and the thrill of reaching new heights.
