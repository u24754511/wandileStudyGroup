Background and Biography

Mary Modiba was born in Polokwane, Limpopo, and completed her schooling in Haenertsburg before moving to Pretoria for university. She is currently pursuing a BIT in Information Systems at the University of Pretoria. She chose this degree because she believes Data Science is the future and wants to play a role in transforming raw data into meaningful insights.

Hobbies

Mary is a storyteller at heart. She spends her free time writing short stories, capturing unique ideas and characters in her notebooks. She also has a love for baking, experimenting with new recipes and perfecting her signature desserts. When she wants to challenge her mind, she enjoys solving puzzles, seeing them as a fun and engaging way to keep her problem-solving skills sharp.
