Background and Biography

Aphiwe Mdletshe was born in Johannesburg, Gauteng, and moved to Pretoria for his tertiary education. He is currently studying BIT in Information Systems at the University of Pretoria. His interest in technology was sparked by his curiosity about how applications are built, leading him to explore programming and software development. He is passionate about creating innovative solutions that make everyday life more efficient.

Hobbies

Aphiwe enjoys competitive sports, particularly soccer, where he plays as a forward in local tournaments. He also has a deep appreciation for music and spends his free time playing the guitar and composing songs. When he's not on the field or strumming chords, he enjoys watching sci-fi movies and analyzing their futuristic concepts.
