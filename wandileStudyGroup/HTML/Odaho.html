Background and Biography

Odaho Mudau was born in Thohoyandou, Limpopo. His academic journey brought him to the University of Pretoria, where he is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Technology in Information Systems. Odaho has always been interested in how systems work behind the scenes, especially in the world of business and technology. He chose Information Systems because it aligns perfectly with his analytical mindset and his desire to bring efficiency and innovation to organizations.

Hobbies

Odaho is a strategic thinker who enjoys activities that challenge his mind. He spends his free time playing chess, solving puzzles, and reading about business and finance. When he's looking to unwind, he enjoys listening to music and watching documentaries about emerging technologies and entrepreneurship.
