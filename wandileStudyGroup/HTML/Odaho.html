Background and Biography

Odaho Mudau was born in Thohoyandou, Limpopo, and later moved to Pretoria to study at the University of Pretoria. He is currently enrolled in the BIT in Information Systems program. His fascination with technology began when he first built his own computer as a teenager, which sparked his passion for system design and software development. He believes that technology will shape the future, and he wants to be at the forefront of innovation.

Hobbies

Odaho has a strategic mind, and his love for chess reflects that. He enjoys analyzing complex moves and outthinking his opponents. In addition to chess, he has a passion for photography, using his camera to capture the beauty of landscapes, people, and everyday life. During his downtime, he can often be found reading science fiction novels, immersing himself in futuristic worlds that fuel his imagination.
